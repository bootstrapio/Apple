iMovie for iOS

Make your next masterpiece.
On the same device you shot it with.

Harness the power of iPhone and iPad with a moviemaking app that’s just as powerful. iMovie lets you create movies and Hollywood-style trailers that are simple to make and simply spectacular. And you can edit at resolutions up to stunning 4K on iPhone 6s and iPad Pro. When you’re done, you can watch the final cut anywhere with iMovie Theater. A few taps, a few swipes, and you’re ready for your big premiere. Download iMovie for iOS free at the App Store.

Browse

Go straight to video.

If you have an iPhone or iPad, you probably have a lot of great video. And when you want to do something with it, iMovie lets you cut right to the chase. It’s fun and fast to browse, find your favorite clips, and then share them in seconds.

Make movies

No film school required.

A great movie is right at your fingertips. Just choose the photos and videos you want to use. Then tap and swipe to edit, add effects, and create a soundtrack. With a 64‑bit architecture and 4K support on iPhone 6s and iPad Pro, iMovie makes moviemaking faster and more entertaining than ever. And that, ladies and gentlemen, is a wrap.

  • Themes
  • Special Effects
  • Soundtrack
Great new features for iPhone 6s and iPad Pro.

iMovie is designed to take advantage of the beautiful 4K video you can shoot and edit on your iPhone 6s. In fact, iPhone 6s is so powerful you can smoothly edit two streams of 4K video to create effects like picture-in-picture and split screen. And 3D Touch lets you speed up common iMovie tasks. Simply press the iMovie icon on your Home screen to start a new movie in seconds, or press any project thumbnail for an instant preview.

iMovie and iPad Pro are also an impressive moviemaking team, giving you a full-resolution 1080p HD viewer and a full-height Media browser that puts your video in the spotlight. Desktop-class performance lets you create advanced effects with up to three simultaneous streams of 4K video and export your 4K video at blazing speeds. And accessories like the Smart Keyboard let you use efficient shortcuts to make quick work of your project.

Create trailers

The feel-good trailer of the year.

You have hundreds of videos. And one big dream to make it in the movies. iMovie trailers let you quickly create something fun from all that footage — and make it look like something straight out of Hollywood.

iMovie everywhere

Start editing on location.
And finish in your living room.

iMovie for Mac and iMovie for iOS are designed to work together. So now, for example, you can start a project on your iPhone, continue working on your iPad, and then add the finishing touches on your Mac. And with iMovie Theater, all your finished movies and trailers appear as a beautiful display of movie posters, all in one place. So you’ll always be the star of the show.

Lights. Camera. Reaction.

What’s a movie without an audience? iMovie gives you lots of ways to share with your most loyal fans. Share via Messages or YouTube for instant reactions. Get your movie out there, then sit back and take all the credit.

Extensions for Photos app

iMovie works on location from your photo library.

The iMovie extension for Photos lets you enhance your videos right in the Photos app. Trim your clips and add animated text and video filters. An updated Music browser — featuring one-tap song preview and easier category navigation — lets you add background music to set the scene. You can even multitask on iPad by using Slide Over to quickly check a website while keeping iMovie open, or Split View to reference an email while enhancing your video.
