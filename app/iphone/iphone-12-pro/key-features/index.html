Two new sizes. For more screen than ever.

The 6.1-inch iPhone 12 Pro and 6.7-inch iPhone 12 Pro Max both have a new and larger all-screen display

iPhone 12 Pro Max is about the same size as iPhone 11 Pro Max, but has the largest iPhone display ever

You’ll have plenty of room for pro tasks like editing photos and videos on the fly

The fastest chip ever in a smartphone.

The superpowerful A14 Bionic pushes what’s possible now and is ready for just about anything that comes next

Fastest CPU and GPU of any smartphone chip for huge performance gains across the board

Up to 80% faster Neural Engine and up to 70% faster machine learning accelerators than A13 Bionic

Only a chip this advanced could power new possibilities like 4K Dolby Vision recording

Ultraefficient 5-nanometer design boosts performance while saving energy and battery life

Ultra Wide, Wide, and Telephoto cameras

Wide camera captures 27% better low-light photos and videos on iPhone 12 Pro, 87% better on iPhone 12 Pro Max

iPhone 12 Pro Max features a new 65 mm Telephoto camera for closer close-ups and sensor-shift optical image stabilization to keep shots steady

Superfast 5G.

Faster downloads, higher‑quality video streaming, and lots less lag

More 5G bands than any other smartphone for the broadest 5G coverage worldwide3

Sets you up to take full advantage of 5G network upgrades now and in the future

LiDAR Scanner
for next‑level AR
and beyond.

LiDAR allows virtual objects to be placed instantly and precisely for ultrarealistic AR experiences

Uses light to measure the distance of physical objects and create an accurate depth map of your space in nanoseconds

Lets you do things like transform your room into a rainforest or see exactly how a new sneaker will fit

LiDAR also delivers up to 6x faster autofocus in low light and enables Night mode portraits on the Wide camera

Ceramic Shield with 4x better drop performance.5

Ceramic Shield is tougher than any smartphone glass

It’s made by introducing nano-ceramic crystals — which are harder than most metals — into glass for far greater durability

Our dual‑ion exchange process also protects against nicks, scratches, and everyday wear and tear

Big, bright, beautiful OLED display.

Super Retina XDR display delivers extraordinary brightness, incredible color accuracy, and more pixels6

Our latest OLED display brings high resolution to everything you look at — from text to photos to fiery explosions in movies

2,000,000:1 contrast ratio for true blacks and bright whites

Expansive all-screen design spans from edge to edge, immersing you in your content

Magnets align themselves perfectly every time for faster wireless charging — even with a MagSafe case on

Third-party accessory makers also offer MagSafe-compatible cases, car mounts, chargers, and more

The first camera ever to record in Dolby Vision.

Record 4K HDR video with Dolby Vision — not even movie industry cameras can do that

iPhone 12 Pro jumps from 8- to 10-bit HDR recording, capturing 60x more colors for much more lifelike video

Watch your Dolby Vision–recorded content in all its glory on the brilliant OLED display of iPhone 12 Pro

Edit 4K Dolby Vision video and wirelessly stream it to your Apple TV or smart TV to see all that color and detail on the big screen

There’s no phone like iPhone.

Protects your privacy.

Privacy is built into iPhone from the ground up. Face ID data doesn’t leave your iPhone. iOS shows you an app’s privacy practices before you download it. When you use Apple Pay, your card number isn’t shared with merchants. The list goes on.

Learn more about Apple and privacy

Everything just works.

Our hardware and software work together seamlessly. Want to pair new AirPods with your iPhone? It’s a simple one‑tap setup. Want to share photos, videos, or contacts with friends nearby? AirDrop lists their names onscreen, so you can choose with a tap.

Designed to last.

Superstrong materials and water resistance make iPhone incredibly durable. And automatic iOS updates deliver new features and security enhancements that keep your iPhone running beautifully and help it hold its value longer than other smartphones.

Lighter on the planet.

We’re always innovating to reduce our carbon footprint. Smaller boxes. More boxes per shipment. Recycled materials. Renewable energy. Efforts like these have allowed us to eliminate over 2 million metric tons of carbon emissions annually. And we’re not done yet.
