Features of iPhone 11

All-day
battery life.

Advanced hardware and software work together seamlessly to help you get incredible battery life.1 And with an 18W fast-charge adapter (sold separately), you can go from zero to up to 50 percent in 30 minutes.2

The fastest chip ever in a smartphone.

A13 Bionic makes everything feel fast and fluid, from capturing the highest-quality video in a smartphone to unleashing unbelievable AR effects and experiences.

Photos that go from Wide to Ultra Wide.

Add a lot more to your photos — more friends, more skyline, more everything. The Ultra Wide camera captures four times more scene. It’s like taking a step back, even when you can’t take a step back.

Portrait mode.
For beautiful shots with depth.

Portrait mode creates a beautiful depth of field so your subjects really pop, and you can blur the background as much or as little as you like. You can also choose from six studio-quality lighting effects to do things like add directional lighting, or turn snapshots into studio-style monochromes.

Slo-mo for selfies.

The TrueDepth camera takes dramatic slow-motion selfie videos. And it automatically zooms out to fit more into still photos when you turn your iPhone to landscape.

Designed to take a spill and a splash.

iPhone 11 is water resistant to 2 meters for up to 30 minutes.3 And it has the toughest glass in any smartphone — on both sides.
