Smart Battery Case

The all-new Smart Battery Case for iPhone 6s gives you extra battery life with the look and protection of an Apple-designed case. Combined with the Smart Battery Case, iPhone 6s provides up to 18 hours of battery life for browsing the web and up to 20 hours of watching HD video.1 The Smart Battery Case works intelligently with your iPhone to charge simultaneously when you’re plugged in, so both case and iPhone will have more than enough battery life to get you through the day, and then some. The soft elastomer hinge and one-piece design make it easy to put the case on and take it off. You can also connect it to any Lightning-equipped accessory, such as the Lightning Dock. And iOS 9 allows you to see the battery status of your iPhone 6s, your Smart Battery Case, and even your Apple Watch, all in one place.2

Lightning Dock

Use the iPhone Lightning Dock to charge and sync any iPhone with a Lightning connector. Your iPhone sits upright in the dock as it syncs or charges, so it’s perfect for a desk or countertop. Even when your iPhone is in an Apple-designed case, it’s easy to dock. And you can unlock iPhone or use Touch ID without having to remove it from the dock. The iPhone Lightning Dock also features an audio port, so you can use headphones or connect your iPhone to powered speakers while it’s charging.
