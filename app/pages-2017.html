Pages

Documents that stand apart.
Created together.

Pages is a powerful word processor that lets you create documents that look beautiful and read beautifully. And with real-time collaboration, your team can work together on a Mac, iPad, or iPhone — even on a PC using iWork for iCloud.

CollaborationWork with your whole team.
Wherever they are.

Pages lets you collaborate with one person or many people to create and edit documents. And everyone can work at the same time, from across town or on the other side of the world.

See who you’re working with.

While you’re editing, a separate list lets you quickly see who else is in the document.

View changes in real time.

Pages lets you read your collaborators’ edits in a document as they make them — and lets them watch as you make yours. Jump to anyone’s cursor simply by selecting a name in the collaborator list.

No matter what devices you work with,
you can all work together.

You don’t work in one place on just one device. The same goes for Pages. So the documents your team creates using a Mac or iPad look the same on an iPhone or web browser — and vice versa.

PC users can join in, too.

With iWork for iCloud, you and your team can access all your documents in any Mac or PC web browser. Just sign in to iCloud.com, open Pages, and start editing.

Simply amazing results.

From the start, Pages places you in the perfect creative environment. It puts all the right tools in all the right places, so it’s easy to choose a look, customize fonts, personalize text styles, and add beautiful graphics. And everyone collaborating on a document has access to the same powerful features.

Start with a great-looking template.

Choose from 60 beautiful templates that make your document look professional from the get-go.

See your progress.

Easily track comments, monitor changes, and add highlights. Your edits are saved automatically.

Communicate beautifully.

Add charts, shapes, tables, or math equations. Drop in a photo, resize it, rotate it, and remove the background. Create subscripts and superscripts. And have text automatically wrap around an image.

Jump to anywhere in your document.

Add bookmarks to quickly link one part of your document to another.

Unlock documents with a touch.

Touch ID makes it a snap to open password-protected files on supported iOS devices as well as on MacBook Pro models with the Touch Bar.

Microsoft Word friendly.

Teaming up with someone who uses Microsoft Word? Pages makes it simple. You can save Pages documents as Word files. Or import and edit Word documents right in Pages. Most popular Word features are supported, too. Now it’s no problem to work on the same project. Even if you use different apps.
